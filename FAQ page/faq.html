What is eChannelling ?

eChannelling is a service that offers convenient booking facilities for Doctor Appointments at island wide prominent hospitals in Sri Lanka.

How can I use the Doctor booking service through eChannelling?

  • Call +94 753178064 from any mobile network to channel your doctor.
  • Visit SLEdoctor.lk
  • Download the eChannelling mobile app from Google play store or App Store
  • Visit Banks islandwide (Refer Banks)

How to download eChannelling Mobile Application?

The Mobile Application can be downloaded via Google play store or App store.

How do I search a doctor using eChannelling?

You can use any one option below

  • Option 01 : Type the doctor's first name and search OR
  • Option 02 : Select the available hospital and search

How to book an appointment with a doctor?

Select the preferred date (required session) of the doctor Enter patient details Select the preferred payment method and proceed. You have maximum of 10 minutes to complete.
